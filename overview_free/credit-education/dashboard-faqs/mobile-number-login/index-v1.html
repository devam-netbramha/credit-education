Mobile Number Based Login FAQs

Why is my account locked?

Your account may be locked for security reasons if our system detects unusual activity. This can happen if we notice anything out of the ordinary with your recent login attempts or transactions. It’s our way of ensuring your account stays secure.

What should I do?

Use a Trusted Device and Network: Try logging in from a device and network you’ve used before, like your home Wi-Fi/mobile ISP or personal computer/mobile device.

Avoid Public Networks: If you’re using a public or shared network, switch to a more secure connection, as public networks can sometimes trigger security measures.

Clear Browser Cache and Cookies: If you’re using a web browser, clearing your cache and cookies might help resolve the issue.

Ensure Your Device is Secure: Make sure your device’s operating system and browser are up to date and run a security scan to check for malware.

Disable VPNs or Proxies: If you’re using a VPN or proxy, try disabling it temporarily, as these can sometimes cause your activity to appear suspicious.

If you’re still unable to access your account, please contact our support team on contact us or +91 - 22 - 6140 4300 for further assistance.
